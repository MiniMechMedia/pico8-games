Build an epic sandwich

Build the tallest sandwich you can by catching the falling ingredients. The game ends when you complete your sandwich by catching a piece of bread. In Free Play you can have extra bread in your sandwich - the game only ends when you press X.

Controls

  • Arrow Keys - Move your plate
  • X - Start a new game when you finish the game. Also ends the game when you are in Free Play mode
  • P - Bring up pause menu to turn on/off Free Play
