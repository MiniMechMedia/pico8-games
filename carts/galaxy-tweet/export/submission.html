A tweetable galaxy simulator

A galaxy simulation inspired by https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Density_wave_theory.

This cart is tweetable at just 277 characters ::_:: cls() srand() for i = 1, 100 do pset(rnd(128),rnd(128),7) end for i = 1, 1800 do r=rnd(50) p=r/30 a=rnd() - t()/(2+r*r/50) x = r*cos(a) y = r *1.3 * sin(a) pset(64 + x * cos(p) - y*sin(p), 64 + x * sin(p) + y * cos(p), rnd({7,7,7,7,7,7,7,15,10})) end flip() goto _
