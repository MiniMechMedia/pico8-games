Don't let Big Water shut you down!

You have invented a weather machine that will bring rain to the desert. But Big Water has an army of lawyers, judges, and politicians to try to make you turn it off. Use your water towers to soak their legal documents and keep operating.

Controls

  • Mouse - move cursor
  • Click - pick up / place tower

Hints

  • You can only place one extra tower per wave
