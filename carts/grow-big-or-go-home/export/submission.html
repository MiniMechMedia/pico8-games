Eat the other microbes to grow big...or be eaten yourself!

Under the microscope it's eat or be eaten. Eat smaller microbes to make yourself bigger. But don't touch anything that's bigger than you are! Grow big enough and you will win the game.

Controls

  • Arrow keys - move
  • X - start a new game when the game ends
