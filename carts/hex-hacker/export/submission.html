Q: How do you defeat an evil hex? A: Use a hex editor

An evil wizard put a hex on you, trapping you in a tower. Use a hex [editor] of your own to hack your way through the challenges. It will take drastic measures to break out once and for all.

Controls

  • Arrow Keys - move player, navigate menu
  • X - interact with object, close menu
