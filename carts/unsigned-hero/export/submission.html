Fight your way higher and higher to the 255th level of the dungeon

Fight your way higher and higher through the dungeon. Make it to the 255th level to escape! Defeat all the enemies on a floor and you will ascend up a level. Die and you will go deeper down a level.

Controls

  • X - Attack
  • Arrow Keys - Move

Hints

Failure is a stepping stone to success
